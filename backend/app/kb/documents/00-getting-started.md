# Getting Started with the Platform

## Welcome

Welcome to our cyber training platform! This guide will help you get started quickly.

## Account Setup

To create an account, follow these steps:

1. Visit the login page
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (minimum 12 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters)
5. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
6. Complete your profile information

## First Login

On your first login, you will:
- Be taken to the dashboard
- See available training modules
- Be able to set your learning preferences
- Access the knowledge base

## User Roles

The platform supports different user roles:

- **Trainee**: Entry-level users learning fundamentals
- **Operator**: Users managing day-to-day operations
- **Instructor**: Users creating and managing training content
- **Support Engineer**: Users handling technical support tickets
- **Admin**: Full platform access and management

Your role determines what features and modules you can access.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard shows:
- Recent training modules
- Your progress and achievements
- Pending tickets (if applicable)
- Quick access to commonly used tools
- System status and announcements

## Getting Help

If you need help:
- Check the knowledge base (you're reading it!)
- Contact support via the help button
- Watch tutorial videos in the learning center
- Join community forums

## Next Steps

After setup, we recommend:
1. Complete your profile
2. Take the platform orientation module
3. Explore available training modules
4. Set learning goals
